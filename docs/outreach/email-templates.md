## Key Messaging Rules

1. **Lead with the outcome, not the feature.** "2 Stunden weniger Verwaltung pro Tag" beats "Zero-Knowledge-Verschlüsselung"
2. **Always mention the 30-day free trial.** It's the #1 conversion tool — zero risk for them.
3. **Mention the Gründerpreis.** "Exklusiv für die ersten 20 Praxen" creates urgency without pressure.
4. **Mention the AI consent explainer.** Patients understand what they sign — in their own language. No competitor has this.
5. **Keep it under 100 words.** Doctors scan, they don't read.

## Legal Note (UWG §7)

German law (UWG §7) is stricter than GDPR for cold email. To minimize risk:
1. Only send to professional practice email addresses (not personal)
2. Include an unsubscribe mechanism in every email
3. Document your legitimate interest assessment
4. Keep the list small and targeted (10 practices, not 1000)
5. Stop immediately if someone requests no further contact
6. Have your AVV (Auftragsverarbeitungsvertrag) ready — practices will ask for it before any trial
